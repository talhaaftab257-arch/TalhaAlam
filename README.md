### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm **Talha**, an AI/ML Engineer and Data Analyst/BI Developer with **4+ years of industry experience**, based in Pakistan and actively working across Pakistan, Malaysia, UAE, and remote-first teams. I specialize in **LLM Agents, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems, computer vision, and MLOps**, and I build AI solutions that turn raw data into decisions organizations can act on. Alongside AI engineering, I bring strong **BI development** chops — Power BI, Microsoft Fabric, and Databricks/PySpark — so I can take a system from raw pipeline to executive dashboard.

- 🔭 **Currently building:** [ASDT (Autonomous Software Development Team)](https://github.com/TalhaAlam1) — a multi-agent AI orchestration platform coordinating **12 specialized agents** through LangGraph, with Pydantic-validated JSON contracts, Postgres-backed checkpointing, and full Docker/Kubernetes deployment.
- 🌱 **Currently learning:** deeper agentic architecture patterns, scalable MLOps pipelines, and advanced prompt/context engineering for multi-agent systems.
- 💼 **Open to:** AI/ML Engineer, Data Analyst/BI Developer, and LLM/RAG Engineer roles — Pakistan, Malaysia (Employment Pass), UAE, or remote.
- ⚡ **Fun fact:** My academic background is actually in Physics (BSc, GC University Lahore) — I got into ML chasing the same kind of pattern-finding that drew me to physics in the first place.
- 📫 **Reach me:** via [LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/in/talhaaftab11) or my [portfolio site](https://talha-portfolio-ten.vercel.app).
